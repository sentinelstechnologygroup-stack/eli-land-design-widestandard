Vargos on the Lake

Vargos on the Lake

Location: Houston, TX

Services: Landscape Architecture & Landscape Construction

Vargos on the Lake is a Class-A 500 unit apartment and townhome community situated in the Piney Point area of Houston Texas. The landscape design of Vargos on the Lake blends the clean lines of the architecture and outdoor spaces with the existing beauty of the lake and mature trees. The crisp lines of the pool deck, terraces and interior courtyard complements the contemporary lines of the building. Pockets and beds of landscaping throughout the amenities and property provide softness and a connection to the sites natural features. Amenities on the project include outdoor kitchens, a resort style pool, dog parks and multiple terraces for open recreation.

About us
