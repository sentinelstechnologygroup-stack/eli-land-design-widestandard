Landscape Portfolio

E.L.I. Portfolio

Landscape Architecture is the link that ties architecture into its surroundings. It is the visual that screens the essential piping systems, and landscape architecture is the voice that speaks for Mother Nature. Landscape Architecture is an enormous puzzle of architecture, civil engineering, topography, existing trees, soil types, amenities, pedestrian spaces, sustainability, code requirements, budget, and aesthetics. The numerous pieces of information are put together to form a cohesive landscape that is not only beautiful when completed, but also functional.

About us

E.L.I land design specializes in the planning, design, and installation of commercial and residential landscapes in The Woodlands / Houston area. E.L.I. delights in our ability to observe the balance between architecture, landscape, the interests of our clients and the natural character of the site. Thoughtful design and an impeccable execution, result in unique outdoor spaces that engage and inspire.
